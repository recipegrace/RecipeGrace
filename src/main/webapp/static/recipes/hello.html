Apple Butter #2

Credit
Ferosh Jacob
Categories

Measure Ingredient name and notes
4 lbs apples
2 cups apple cider
1/2 sugar ,less than recipe
2 1/8 tbsp cinnamon, much more
2 tsp cloves
1 can use cinnamon sticks

Put peeled, cored and sliced apples in heavy saucepan with cider and cook until soft. Force thru food mill. Measure pulp into kettle and for each cup add 1/4 cup sugar. Add remaining ingredients and cook over low heat until less than half the original volume remains and it is thick. Appearance will be dark brown.. Can be frozen for the year.

Ingredients can easily be changed - added or subtracted - according to individual taste.
